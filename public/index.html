What is this? It's a sequence of beats in time. Every sequence is divided into 16 ticks. Black cells are beats, white cells are rests. The longer the cell, the more time it takes.

What do I do? Press space to play the sequence, press space again to stop. Click on a beat to tell the system that it should be a rest instead. Conversely, click on a rest to tell the system there should be a beat there instead. Then press Next to get a fresh sequence.
